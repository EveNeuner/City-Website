    Eataly

    Eataly, located in the Prudential Center in Boston, has some of the best
    food in the city. Filled with many different eateries, markets, dining,
    and groceries, there are good options for lot's of different needs.

    The Q Restaurant

    Located in Chinatpwn in Boston, the Q has a wide variety of authentic
    Japanese and Chinese cuisine. Their speciality is their hot pots, which
    they have many options for. The experience is completed by the restaurant's
    bright, modern atmosphere.

    Luke's Lobster Portland Pier

    Luke's Lobster, located in Portland Maine, has some of the best Lobster
    in the city. Lobster, and lobster rolls, is the dish of Maine, and is
    important of a complete New England experience.
