    USS Constitution Museum

    The USS Constitution Museum is an impressive replica of the real ship.
    It is located in the Charlestown Navy Yard Boston Massachusetts, and is
    part of the National Historical Park. Visitors can explore the ship,
    learn about it's history, and connect with the past.

    Boston Museum of Fine Arts

    The Boston Museum of Fine Arts, or the BMFA, is the 20th largest art museum
    in the world, and is home to more than 450,000 pieces of art. The museum
    contains exibits feauturing paintings, statues, artifacts, and more.
